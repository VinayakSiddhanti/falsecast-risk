FALSECAST: Rice Blast Risk Forecast System

FALSECAST is a decision-support tool that predicts the risk of rice blast disease based on key weather parameters such as humidity, temperature, and rainfall. It helps farmers and researchers make informed decisions about fungicide applications and crop management.
